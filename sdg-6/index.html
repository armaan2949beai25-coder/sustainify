About SDG 6

Why Clean Water Matters

Water is the foundation of life. SDG 6 aims to ensure everyone has access to clean water and adequate sanitation by 2030.

🌊

Universal Access

Achieve universal and equitable access to safe and affordable drinking water for all people worldwide.

🚿

Sanitation & Hygiene

Achieve access to adequate and equitable sanitation and hygiene for all, ending open defecation.

♻️

Water Quality

Improve water quality by reducing pollution, eliminating dumping, and minimizing release of hazardous chemicals.

💧

Water Efficiency

Substantially increase water-use efficiency across all sectors and ensure sustainable withdrawals of freshwater.

🏔️

Ecosystem Protection

Protect and restore water-related ecosystems including mountains, forests, wetlands, rivers, and lakes.

🤝

Global Cooperation

Expand international cooperation and capacity-building in water and sanitation-related activities and programmes.
