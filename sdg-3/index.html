SDG 3 Targets

Reduce Maternal Mortality

Aim to reduce global maternal mortality ratio to less than 70 per 100,000 live births.

End Epidemics

End the epidemics of AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria, and tropical diseases.

Mental Health

Promote mental health and well-being, and reduce premature mortality.

Universal Healthcare

Achieve universal health coverage, including financial risk protection.

Reduce Road Injuries

Halve the number of global deaths and injuries from road traffic accidents.

Disease Awareness

COVID-19

Symptoms: Fever, cough, fatigue.

Prevention: Vaccination, hygiene, masks.

Malaria

Symptoms: Chills, fever, sweating.

Prevention: Mosquito nets, repellents.

Tuberculosis

Symptoms: Chronic cough, weight loss.

Prevention: BCG vaccine, ventilation.

Dengue Fever

Symptoms: High fever, rash, muscle pain.

Prevention: Eliminate stagnant water.

CPR Basics

Push hard and fast in the center of the chest (100-120 beats per min).

Helplines

First Aid
