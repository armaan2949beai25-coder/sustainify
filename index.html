What are the SDGs?

The Sustainable Development Goals are a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity.

Global Partnership

193 Member States committed to this shared blueprint for peace and prosperity for people and the planet.

Comprehensive Action

17 integrated goals recognizing that action in one area will affect outcomes in others.

Measurable Targets

169 targets to track progress and ensure no one is left behind in the journey to 2030.
