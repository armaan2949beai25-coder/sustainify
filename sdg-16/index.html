Pillars of SDG 16

The foundation of a sustainable and equitable global society.

🕊️

Peace & Non-Violence

Significantly reduce all forms of violence and related death rates everywhere, ending abuse and exploitation.

⚖️

Justice & Rule of Law

Promote the rule of law at the national and international levels and ensure equal access to justice for all.

🏛️

Strong Institutions

Develop effective, accountable and transparent institutions at all levels to combat corruption and bribery.

🤝

Equality & Inclusion

Ensure responsive, inclusive, participatory and representative decision-making at all levels.

Pathways to Progress

How we can work together to achieve global harmony and justice.

  • Promote Transparency: Support open data initiatives and demand accountability from local governments.
  • Protect Human Rights: Stand up against discrimination and advocate for the vulnerable and marginalized.
  • Combat Corruption: Refuse bribery and support organizations dedicated to ethical business practices.
  • Engage in Democracy: Participate in local elections, community forums, and civic decision-making processes.
  • Support Legal Identity: Advocate for policies that provide free and accessible birth registration for everyone.
