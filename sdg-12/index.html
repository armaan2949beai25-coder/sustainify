What is SDG 12?

Sustainable Development Goal 12 is about doing more and better with less. It is about decoupling economic growth from environmental degradation, increasing resource efficiency and promoting sustainable lifestyles.

Consumption and production drive the global economy, but they also rest on the use of the natural environment and resources in a model that continues to lead to destructive impacts on the planet.

The Root Causes

Why is our current model unsustainable?

Linear Economy

The "take-make-dispose" model creates massive waste and rapidly depletes natural resources.

Overproduction

Producing more goods than needed leads to excess inventory, energy waste, and pollution.

Fast Fashion & Consumerism

A culture of buying cheap, short-lived products increases landfill mass and resource extraction.

Global Solutions

Circular Economy

Designing products to be reused, repaired, or recycled rather than thrown away.

Sustainable Supply Chains

Ensuring transparency and ethical sourcing of materials across all industries.

Conscious Consumerism

Individuals choosing eco-friendly products and minimizing personal food and material waste.
